What is RL Inbox?

The Email Inbox RL Environment simulates the daily decision-making process of an email user. Every email is treated as an environment state, and every decision—whether to classify, archive, or delete—is an action.

Our system uses a deterministic reward logic aligned with real-world productivity goals. Correct decisions earn positive reinforcement, while mistakes are penalized, allowing agents (and humans) to learn the most efficient paths to Inbox Zero.

Spam Detection

Robust identification of unwanted emails. Learn to separate genuine communication from harmful spam with high accuracy.

Priority Ranking

Identify critical updates from bosses or clients. Prioritize what matters most to maintain high productivity.

Smart Organization

Master the art of categorization. Automatically move emails to appropriate folders based on content and sender heuristics.
